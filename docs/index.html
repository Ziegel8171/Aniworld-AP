How It Works

Get started in under a minute

1

Install the Extension

Click "Add to Firefox" to install AniWorld AP directly from Firefox Add-ons. It's free and takes just one click.

2

Watch Anime on AniWorld

Navigate to aniworld.to and start watching any anime episode. The extension automatically detects the anime and episode.

3

Skip Automatically

Enable auto-skip in the settings popup, and the extension will automatically skip intros and outros. Or use the manual skip buttons.

4

Customize Your Experience

Adjust settings like marker colors, skip offset, playback memory duration, and more to match your preferences.
